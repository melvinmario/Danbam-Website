Our History

Danbam is a traditional Korean food restaurant that was established in 2010 in Itaewon, South Korea. Danbam started as the leading brand for skirt meat. Founded by Park SaeRoyi, an entrepreneur who started his business career since 2008, Danbam has expanded 8 branches all over Korea.


Danbam presents the original taste of Korean BBQ and traditional Korean food that packed in modern way for everyone. Now you can also taste what Korean royal perfection is all about.
